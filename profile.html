Corporate Profile

Leading the Future of Power Conversion & Smart Charging

Our Identity

ECharging New Energy is a high-tech enterprise founded in 2014, specializing in special and new technologies and deeply engaged in the application field of new energy power supply systems.

Leveraging the integration of internet technology and power automation, ECharging has taken the lead in providing multi-modal solutions that integrate charging station operations, photovoltaic storage, and vehicle energy transfer services.

World-Class R&D Heritage

Our R&D team is composed of experts from renowned domestic and international companies such as Huawei and Emerson. This elite background forms our strong technological development and continuous innovation capabilities, placing our product development at the industry's forefront.
