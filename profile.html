Corporate Profile

Professional New Energy Power Supply System Solutions

Our Identity

ECharging New Energy is a high-tech enterprise founded in 2014, specializing in special and new technologies and deeply engaged in the application field of new energy power supply systems.

Our R&D team is composed of experts from renowned domestic and international companies such as Huawei and Emerson. The company’s new product development capability is among the leading in the industry in China, forming strong technological development and continuous innovation capabilities.

Leveraging the integration of internet technology and power automation technology, ECharging has taken the lead in providing electric vehicle operators with multi‑modal solutions that integrate charging station operations, energy storage, and photovoltaic storage services.
